Silver Psych

Indica

Details

Silver Psych is our very own house strain and a GVO favorite indica strain, known for its potent aroma and effects. This strain sports a stinky combination of hoppy, earthy, gassy smells with hints of pine and cinnamon that is only indicative of the heady, sedative high that is to come. The notable aromas come from legendary terpene percentages - averaging over 4% total and often testing over 5%. This kiefy strain has anti-inflammatory properties and is helpful for appetite stimulation, insomnia, and stress.
