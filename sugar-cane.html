Sugar Cane

Hybrid

Details

Sugar Cane is a soothing, sweet smelling hybrid strain with an aromatic balance of earthy undertones and a hint of spice. Known to produce a relaxed, yet motivated state of mind, the effects of Sugar Cane can help alleviate the symptoms of stress, depression, and chronic fatigue. It can also benefit those who may be suffering from appetite loss, nausea, chronic pain, headaches or migraines.
