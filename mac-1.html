M.A.C. 1

Hybrid

Details

M.A.C. 1, also known as "The MAC," is a versatile hybrid strain that has become popularized for its upbeat and balancing effects. Users seeking remedy for pain, appetite loss, and inflammation often turn towards this strain. The buds are one of a kind: dense, nearly white, and saturated with milky trichomes. The flavor is smooth and funky, blending a spicy and citrusy aroma with floral and piney undertones.
