Dark Ghosttrain

Sativa

Details

Dark Ghosttrain is a mentally stimulating sativa strain that is coupled with elements of pain-relief and relaxation. This strain features soft, dark green buds adorned with orange and red hairs and a high testing terpene profile, which strengthens this strain's flavors and effects. Users can expect a delectably fresh and sweet floral flavor, blended with more subtle gassy and citrusy aromas. Dark Ghosttrain can help with fatigue, stress, pain, and inflammation, and offers users a way to perk up after a long day.
