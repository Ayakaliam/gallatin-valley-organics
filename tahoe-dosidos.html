Tahoe OG x Dosidos

Indica

Details

Tahoe OG x Dosidos is a stoney indica with a sedative buzz that melts over the body and helps users reduce pain, inflammation, nausea, and muscle spasms. This is the perfect rainy day strain, as it embodies the relaxation and heavy body sensations of an indica with an added kick of euphoria and the giggles. The flavor is sweet and earthy, with an aroma that exudes a floral funk and pop of pine.
