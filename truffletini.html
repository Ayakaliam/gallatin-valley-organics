Truffletini

Indica

Details

Truffletini is an uplifting indica strain that has sticky lime green buds covered in orange hairs, with a refreshing citrus aroma with hints of pine and cinnamon to match. Users often choose this strain to help manage pain, nausea, stress, and muscle spasms. Although this strain offers relaxing qualities of an indica, the dominant terpene limonene invites an uplifting element to the high, encouraging creativity, euphoria, and even a social buzz.
