Orangeade

Hybrid

Details

Orangeade is a fragrant, citrusy smelling hybrid strain with a strong tangerine aroma and subtle floral flavor. Orangeade is known for its mood-lifting properties, making it useful for treating the symptoms associated with anxiety or depression. This strain has also been found to support appetite suppression, due to high levels of its most dominant terpene, humulene.
