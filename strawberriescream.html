Strawberries and Cream

Details

Strawberries & Cream is a sativa strain with a pungent, fresh berry scent that combines subtle spicy overtones to create its signature “strawberry cheesecake” aroma. Effects can induce feelings of happiness, creativity, and physical relaxation with little to no sedation, which can help manage issues with chronic fatigue, depression, and chronic stress. Strawberries & Cream can also be effective for the treatment of headaches, migraines, feelings of nausea, and/or appetite loss.
