Silvertip

indica

Details

This sativa-leaning cannabis plant sprouts buds that release a curious mix of diesel and sweet blueberries tossed together with a bit of skunky-ness. After the exhale, you might find a well-blended taste of pine and earth lingering on your palette. and grape flavor. The aroma is more spicy and herbal accented by notes of fresh grapefruit.
