Wedding Crasher

Indica

Details

Wedding Crasher is a mellow, sugary-smelling indica strain that features notes of vanilla and grape with a sharp, gassy finish. Effects make Wedding Crasher perfect for treating depression, chronic stress, mood swings, insomnia, nausea, appetite loss and inflammation.
