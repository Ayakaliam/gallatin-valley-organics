Cookies and Cream

Hybrid

Details

Cookies & Cream is an uplifting hybrid strain, with effects that will leave users feeling euphoric, creative, and relaxed. With a flavor profile that is sweet and fruity, with hints of vanilla and spice, this strain is sure to satisfy anyone with a sweet tooth. Cookies & Cream features buds with a light purple hue, bright orange hairs, and frosty trichomes that provide a terpene profile that may aid users in treating conditions such as chronic stress, anxiety, nausea, insomnia, muscle spasms, and depression. In smaller doses, this strain can offer a functional and long-lasting floaty buzz, but higher doses can soothe users into an early night’s sleep.
