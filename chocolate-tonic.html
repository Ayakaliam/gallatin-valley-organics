Chocolate Tonic is a hybrid CBD:THC 1: 1 strain that has a balanced cerebral and body high that is ideal for managing pain and reducing symptoms associated with major medical ailments. The cerebral high is uplifting yet grounding, which can help users feel more in control of their headspace and offset anxiety, depression, and stress. The accompanying effects of CBD have been shown to treat epilepsy, migraines, and opioid addiction, as well as reduce inflammation, PTSD symptoms, and even protect against neurological diseases. Chocolate Tonic has a unique flavor and aroma, highlighting a fusion between chocolate and pine.
