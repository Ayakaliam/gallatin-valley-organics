Kandy Kush

Hybrid

Details

Candy Kush is a potent sativa-dominant hybrid with uplifting and motivating effects. This buzzy strain highlights a flavor and aroma that is sweet like candy, with strong notes of lemon and more subtle hints of cinnamon, hops, and lavender. Candy Kush has been known to help increase productivity, as well as alleviate symptoms of nausea, pain, and muscle spasms.
