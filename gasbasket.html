Gas Basket

Hybrid

Details

Gas Basket is an indica-dominant hybrid strain that will leave users feeling relaxed, happy, and soothed. True to its name, this strain sports a gassy and cheesy flavor, with notes of berries and vanilla. This flower has fluffy, dark green buds, adorned with orange hairs and bright white trichomes. With an elated buzz that starts in the head and settles into the body, this strain can help symptoms like stress, depression, pain, and insomnia slip away.
