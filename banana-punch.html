Banana Punch

Indica

Details

Banana Punch is a potent indica strain that produces sedative effects felt from head-to-toe. The effects of this strain come on slow and strong, washing away pain and stress. This strain has also been known to alleviate symptoms of insomnia and inflammation. Banana Punch has an earthy and tropical aroma with notes of pineapple, citrus, and banana.
