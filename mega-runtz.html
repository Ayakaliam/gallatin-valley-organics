Mega Runtz

Details

Mega Runtz is a buzzy, uplifting hybrid strain that offers a complex blend of funky, fruit-based aromas paired with sharp, diesel undertones. It has been found to be helpful in managing the symptoms of anxiety, treating migraines, and regulating issues with nausea. MegaRuntz has also been popularized for its social benefits, as it is known to produce feelings of euphoria, contentment, and well-being.
