Cherry Bomb

Hybrid

Details

Cherry Bomb is an sativa-dominant hybrid with a musky and piney berry flavor that offers users a mellow, functional high. This strain is ideal for relieving pain, inflammation, mood disorders, and muscle spasms during the daytime, as its effects are relaxing yet not sedative. Cherry Bomb also contains special neuroprotective terpenes which can aid in preventing neurodegenerative conditions.
