New Patient

Follow these step-by-step instructions or contact us for help.

First, you need to be 18+ years old and have proof of a debilitating medical condition that is recognized by the state of Montana.

Once you have obtained medical documentation verifying your debilitating condition, you will need to schedule a doctor's appointment for your information to be reviewed and for medical marijuana to be recommended as a treatment.

Once you receive your recommendation, follow the link in step 2 to apply with the State of Montana

If you are ready to schedule an appointment for yourself, here is one office in Bozeman we recommend:

MMJCARD Application

Once you've received a doctor's recommendation, you will fill out an online application with the state of Montana.

Please follow the link below to register and obtain your MMJ Card. You will need to upload a photo of yourself and a copy of your driver's license, state ID, tribal ID, or passport. Once approved, you will be issued a temporary medical card. This needs be to printed out an brought in with photo ID for sale.
