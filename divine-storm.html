Divine Storm

Indica

Details

Divine Storm is a smooth smoking indica strain with a diesel inhale and sweet exhale. The effects of this strain are felt quickly, bringing a wave of relaxation and pain relief over the whole body. The strong body high is often followed by a notable uplift in mood. This strain is ideal for treating insomnia, nausea, and anxiety, as well as being an effective analgesic. A pungent gassy aroma is released as the bud is broken up, and intensifies as the bud is ground up and smoked.

Top Terpenes
